Caramel Flan

Contains: Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Vanilla

Learn how to make a wonderful 4 ingredient caramel flan in just 30 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 6 eggs
  • 850mL Milk
  • 50-150g Granulated Sugar
  • 1tbsp Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Warm up the milk for 4 minutes in the microwave
  2. Whisk together whole eggs and vanilla extract
  3. Incorporate warm milk slowly and whisk together
  4. Add sugar to desired sweetness
  5. Optional: strain mixture and remove bubbles
  6. Warm up sugar and water in a pan until caramel is light orange
  7. Pour caramel to bottom of flan dish (pyrex recommended)
  8. Pour milk/egg mixture into the dish over the hardened caramel
  9. Bake at 440F for 25-30'
