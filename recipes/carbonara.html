Carbonara

Contains: Eggs, Pork, Cheese, Pepper

Learn how to make a wonderful Carbonara!

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan

Steps:

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Cook pancetta in a pan to render fat, to desired crispiness
  3. Add pepper to pancetta, drain some of the fat
  4. Mix 1-2 egg with desired amount of parmesan in a bowl
  5. Turn off heat in pancetta pan
  6. Add pasta + pasta water to pan (to prevent frying)
  7. Once cooled/warm add egg/cheese mixture
  8. Mix thoroughly
  9. Plate and enjoy!
