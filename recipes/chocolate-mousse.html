Two-Ingredient Chocolate Mousse

Contains: Eggs, Chocolate

Learn how to make a wonderful 2 ingredient chocolate mousse!

Ingredients:

  • 6 eggs
  • 200 grams of baker's chocolate

Steps:

  1. Seperate egg whites and yolks
  2. Melt chocolate over bain-marie
  3. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks
  4. Remove melted chocolate from heat
  5. Slowly incorporate egg yolks and stir with force
  6. Optional: add a small amount of butter, whipping cream, or milk to melting chocolate if consistency is too thick
  7. Take about a quarter of egg whites and mix into chocolate mixture
  8. Take egg white and chocolate mixture and slowly incorporate it into whipped egg whites
  9. Gently fold entire chocolate mixture into egg whites until color is a uniform brown
  10. Transfer into desired dish/containers and let set in the fridge for at least 4 hours
